Why this exists

Contracts lock in expectations. Teams lock in narratives. Reversals become expensive. The moment a deal closes, the cost of being wrong compounds — not in projections, but in execution reality.

AI summarizes markets. Advisors optimize execution. But the upstream question — "Should we do this deal at all?" — rarely has a structured answer before signature. That gap is what this engagement addresses.
